About Asthma & COPD Weather

We are dedicated to providing information about how weather conditions can affect asthma and COPD, and offering practical advice for managing these conditions in various weather scenarios.

Weather Condition Impact on Asthma Impact on COPD
Sunny and Clear May trigger symptoms in some individuals due to high pollen levels and UV exposure. Generally favorable, but high temperatures can exacerbate symptoms in some cases.
Humid May worsen asthma symptoms due to increased mold and dust mite growth. Can worsen COPD symptoms, especially in hot and humid conditions.
Windy May stir up pollen and outdoor allergens, triggering asthma symptoms. Can exacerbate COPD symptoms, especially for those sensitive to changes in air quality.
